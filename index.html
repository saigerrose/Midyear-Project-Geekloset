y2k

Y2k fashion, coming straight from the year 2000, has been very popularized in the most recent years. Consists of clothing popularly worn during the late 90s and early 2000s.

What to Wear:

  • baby tees, tank tops, and tube tops: bonus points for graphic designs and/or sequins & bedazzle
  • low rise bootcut jeans/wide-legged denim jeans - can find from places such as True Religion and Von Dutch
  • velour suits - Juicy Couture sweatsuits
  • cute designer bags: bonus points if they're mini

cyberghetto

The cyberghetto aesthetic is most similar to the y2k aesthetic. It had more of a darker feel to it and incorporates more street wear while sometimes keeping the bright color and bedazzle.

What to Wear:

  • undersized or oversized shirts and sweaters with tons of graphics
  • big, gemmed out belts
  • fitted hats/caps
  • baggy-sized jeans with graphics

subversive

The subversive fashion aesthetic is all about breaking fashion norms.

What to Wear:

  • muted colors - such as white, grey, brown, black, etc.
  • cutouts, slashes, twists, turns, layers, and straps
  • the current trend shows the subversive aesthetic worn with fashions from those like Rick Owens
  • can be incorporated with the cyberghetto aesthetic as well (ex: subversive shoes matched with cyberghetto styled outfits)

baddie / fashion nova / bbl fashion

The "baddie" aesthetic, now more popularly known as "bbl fashion" is an aethetic in which clothes are worn in order to compliment a BBL body which is typically an extreme hourglass figure.

What to Wear:

  • tight-fit clothes-- more on the skimpier side
  • bodycon, cutouts, and mesh
  • colors can vary - usually bright, pastel, and nude colors
  • found from fast fashion brands like "Fashion Nova" and "Pretty Little Thing"

bohemian

An aesthetic which is more hippie influenced.

What to Wear:

  • more loose and colorful clothing
  • a lot of accessories like bracelets, wastebeads, earrings, and necklaces
  • usually worn: a bright cropped top paired with a colorful, full with patterns, and flowy skirt
